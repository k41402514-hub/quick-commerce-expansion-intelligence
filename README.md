#  Quick Commerce Expansion Intelligence Platform

A Business Intelligence project developed using **Python, SQL, and Power BI** to identify expansion-ready districts for quick commerce companies through demographic analysis, digital readiness assessment, and market opportunity scoring.

---

#  Project Overview

This project demonstrates how data analytics can support strategic expansion decisions in the quick commerce industry.

The solution integrates multiple public datasets, performs feature engineering, develops a District Suitability Score (DSS), and presents executive recommendations through interactive Power BI dashboards.

---

# Business Problem

Quick commerce companies must determine where to establish new dark stores while balancing market demand, digital readiness, customer reach, and operational efficiency.

Traditional expansion decisions often rely on intuition instead of data.

This project provides a data-driven framework to identify expansion-ready districts and prioritize rollout strategies.

#  Dashboards

## Dashboard 1 – Executive Overview

Provides a high-level summary of market opportunity through KPIs, state rankings, market tier distribution, and district analysis.

---

## Dashboard 2 – Market Expansion Strategy

Focuses on identifying expansion-ready districts using geographic visualization, digital readiness, addressable population, and expansion coverage.

---

## Dashboard 3 – Recommendation & Validation

Supports executive decision-making through recommendation scoring, expansion funnel, validation matrix, rollout stages, and customer reach estimation.
